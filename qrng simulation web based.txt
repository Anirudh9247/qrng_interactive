Frontend: React.js or Next.js (Modern, great for "Admin Dashboards").

Backend: Python (Flask or FastAPI).

Why Python? Most Quantum Computing SDKs (like IBM’s Qiskit) are Python-based.

Database: PostgreSQL or MongoDB (To store user profiles and logs).

Quantum Source: * Option A: Use IBM Quantum (Qiskit) to run a circuit on a real quantum computer or a simulator.

Option B: Use a simulated quantum backend if you want faster results during development.



Project Architecture

Before you code, you need to understand the flow. Here is a high-level look at how your app will function:

User/Admin Login: Authentication via JWT (JSON Web Tokens).

User Dashboard: Request a random number (specify range/type).

Quantum Engine: The backend sends a job to a Quantum Processor.

Admin Panel: Monitor system health, manage users, and view generation logs.
